About the Team

The James Madison Club Baseball Team is a competitive club consisting of two teams that operate under UREC and participate in the National Club Baseball Association (NCBA). Our D1 team competes in the Mid-Atlantic North conference while our D2 team competes in the Chesapeake South Conference.

Our club is made up of 50-60 members. Games are played in the Fall and Spring semesters. The ultimate goal for each of our two teams is to win its respective conferences and ultimately advance to the Club Baseball World Series.

We practice and play at Long Field at Mauck Stadium, just a short walk from the Quad and former home of the JMU Baseball team.
