Welcome to JMU CBB!

Welcome to the home page of the James Madison University Club Baseball Team! JMU is located in Harrisonburg, Virginia, along I-81 in the Shenandoah Valley. JMU is home to about 20,000 undergraduate students and 2,000 graduate students. JMU Club Baseball is just one of over 425 clubs at James Madison, 52 of which are sports clubs.

If you have any questions or inquries about the team, don't hesitate to reach us through the Contact Us page.
