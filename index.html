Welcome to JMU Club Baseball!

Welcome to the home page of the JMU Club Baseball Team! James Madison University is located in Harrisonburg, Virginia along I-81 in the Shenandoah Valley. JMU is home to about 20,000 undergraduate students and about 2,000 graduate students. JMU Club Baseball is just one of about 450 clubs at James Madison, 52 of which being sports clubs.

If you have any questions or inquries about the team, don't hesitate to reach us through the Contact Us page.
